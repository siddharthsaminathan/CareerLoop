# Interview Playbook Mode
## Auto-Update Your Interview Playbook

When you come back from an interview, just talk about it naturally. The CareerLoop agent will:
1. Detect you're talking about an interview
2. Extract structured data (company, questions, blunders, learnings)
3. Update `interview-prep/interview-playbook.md` automatically
4. Recalculate patterns across all your interviews

### How to Use
Just vent. Say what happened. The agent handles extraction.

> "I just had an interview at Razorpay. They asked me about system design and I messed up the database sharding question..."

The agent will log everything and tell you what patterns are emerging.

### What Gets Tracked
- Every question asked (per company, per round)
- What went well (your strengths)
- Blunders with root cause analysis and fixes
- Learnings for next time
- Follow-up actions with deadlines
- Cross-interview patterns (recurring blunders, common question types)
